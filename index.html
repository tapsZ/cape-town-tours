Our Tours

We offer quite a number of Tours in and around Cape Town, including those specified by you our Clients. Below are the Portfolios of Six of our main Tours.

City Tour

Castle of Good Hope, District Six Museum, Bo Kaap, Table Mountain, Camps bay, Signal Hill are the preferred sights to be visited the itinery can be changed to suit your wishes and is done at your own pace.

Garden Route

Cango Caves, Elephant Park, and Ostrich Farms are a few examples the itinery will be decided between you and the driver, bookings can also be done by the driver.

Cape Peninsula Tour

Hout Bay, Groot Constantia Wine Estate, Penguin Colony are just a few to mention there are many more and different ways to do a Cape Peninsular Tour, however only so much can be seen in one day therefore its best to discuss which sites you prefer to visit with the driver and do it at your own pace.

Cape WineLands

There are many beautiful wine farms in Cape Town and all offer wine tasting some of them offer a bit more click on the icon to see some of the wine farms we’ve selected choose which ones you would like to visit and the driver will take you there.

Safari

There are three destinations Aquila, Fairy Glenn and Buffelsfontein Aquila is the furthest and the most preferred destination, early departure is essential. Bookings can be done by the driver.

Township, Golf and Adventure Tours

Except for township tours bookings are essential there are many beautiful golf courses in Cape Town to choose from, driver can also recommend a few.

Table Mountain

An iconic feature of cape town enjoy some breathtaking views of the city from on top of the mountain.

Camps Bay

One of Cape Town’s most beautiful beaches attracts a large number of foreign visitors as well as South Africans.

Bo Kaap

Known as the Malay quarter a mixture of Dutch and Georgian architecture houses belonged to the slaves were all white before, on declaration of freedom the owners painted them bright colours as an expression of freedom.

Signal Hill

A landmark located next to lions head and Table Mountain where you can enjoy beautiful views of Robben Island and of the city.

Castle Of Good Hope

The oldest building in South Africa build in the 17th century.

District Six Museum

Is a museum of a former inner city residential area and revives the vibrant community that was forced to relocate by the apartheid government.

Oudsthoorn Ostrich Farm

Visit anyone of the ostrich farms, tours are available, restaurants and shopping.

Cango Caves

Situated close to oudshoorn all tours are led by experienced and accredited cave guides.

Knysna Heads

Enjoy a luxury cruise to the Knysna heads it is a must see for first time visitors to Knysna.

Garden of Eden

Is a walking route in the lush green Knysna forest.

Elephant Park Knysna

Founded in 1994 the first elephant orphanage in South Africa.

Mossel Bay Santos Beach

Is a harbor town situated about 400 km from cape town. Santos beach offers all the luxury accommodation and is a stone’s throw away from Mossel bay harbor.

Hout Bay

A lively coastal town and harbor boat rides to seal island are available daily and has a vibrant flea market.

Groot Constantia Wine Estate

One of the oldest wine farms in South Africa. The land was granted to Simon Van Der Stell by the Dutch East India Company in the 17th century.

Simons Town

Home to the South African navy located on the False Bay coastline.

Boulders Beach Penguin Colony

Fall in love with free roaming penguins and enjoy a swim afterwards at Boulders beach.

Cape Point

The most Southern Westerly tip of Africa. A world heritage site.

St James Beach

Colorful huts and a large tidal pool very popular with the locals in summer.

Steenberg Wine and Golf Estate

Boasts a state of the art modern winery, 5 star hotel and 2 award winning restaurants.

Del Aire Wine Estate Spa

Experience the ultimate in luxury, a world leading destination for wine art and hospitality.

Vredenheim Estate

Situated in Stellenbosch offers the big cats park.

Wine Tasting

All Wine Estates offer wine tasting however some do it at certain times. Bookings are essential.

Spier Wine Estate Eagle In Closure

Attracts many tourist has an eagle enclosure also. Situated in Stellenbosch.

Boschendale Wine Estate

Situated in the Drakenstien valley of Franschoek produces award winning red and white wines and is one of the oldest wine farms in that region.

Safari

On arrival breakfast will be served, after breakfast you go on a game drive with an experienced Game-Ranger. The driver waits for you to take you back to Cape Town.

Mzoli Tavern

A popular place in Gugulethu, known for vibrant parties day and night and for the tastiest grilled meat.

Bo Kaap

Also known as the Malay quarter houses are painted bright colours as a declaration of freedom from slavery.

Erinvale Golf Estate

Lays at the foot of the Helderberg mountains overlooking the green suburbs of Somerset West features an 18 hole Gary player designed course.

Westcoast Golf Estate

Langebaan and Atlantic beach golf estates are both impressive designed 18 hole courses by Gary player.

Shark Cage Diving

Booking is essential get up close and personal with one of natures most feared predators.

Bungee Jumping

Bloukrans Bridge is the ultimate thrill the highest commercially operated bungee jump in the world early departure is essential.
